Here's how TakeOut works

TakeOut gives a platform to connect those who want to donate extra food with those who will distribute it to the needy

Advertize Your Food

Got some extra food that could help someone in need? Simply take a picture and answer a few questions to make a post!

Connect

Those parties who are interested in picking up the food you advertized will contact you to make pickup arrangements.

Pickup

Allow the pickup to occur, and easily help those in need without wasting food!
